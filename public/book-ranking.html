Best Romance Books According to Me

My top 4 books of all time


You Deserve Each Other

Book by Sarah Hogle

It's a lovers to enemies to friends to lover kind of book. How could I not love this?! Though I got annoyed at the characters in the first half, the second half was worth enduirng it.. They really deserved each other.


The Love Hypothesis

Novel by Ali Hazelwood

I can't remember how many times I read this book. But definitely remember loving it every time.


The hating game

Book by Sally Thorne

When I first read the books, it seemed like an okay kind of book. But when I re-read it, I fell in love..


The Mindf*ck Series

Book series by S.T. Abby

The FMC is a freaking psychopath serial killer (with a good motive)! What else do I want?
